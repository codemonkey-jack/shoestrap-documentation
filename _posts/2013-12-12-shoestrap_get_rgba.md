The `shoestrap_get_rgba` function has been deprecated since Shoestrap 3.2

It has now moved inside the [`Shoestrap_Color`](/classes/Shoestrap_Color) class.

```php
<?php return Shoestrap_Color::get_rgba( $hex, $opacity, $echo ); ?>
```
