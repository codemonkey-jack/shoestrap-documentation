The `shoestrap_hex_to_hsv` function has been deprecated since Shoestrap 3.2

It has now moved inside the [`Shoestrap_Color`](/classes/Shoestrap_Color) class.

```php
<?php return Shoestrap_Color::hex_to_hsv( $hex ); ?>
```
