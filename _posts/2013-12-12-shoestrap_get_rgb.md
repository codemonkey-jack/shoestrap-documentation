The `shoestrap_get_rgb` function has been deprecated since Shoestrap 3.2

It has now moved inside the [`Shoestrap_Color`](/classes/Shoestrap_Color) class.

```php
<?php return Shoestrap_Color::get_rgb( $hex, $implode ); ?>
```
